## 3. TARGET UMKM BALI (Riset Google Maps)

### Keyword pencarian di Google Maps:
- "warung kopi bali"
- "cafe bali"
- "villa bali"
- "rental motor bali"
- "restaurant bali"
- "barbershop bali"
- "laundry bali"
- "spa bali"

### Filter:
- ⭐ Rating 3.5-4.5 (belum sempurna, butuh branding)
- 📍 Area: Kuta, Seminyak, Canggu, Denpasar, Ubud
- 📸 Foto-foto biasa aja (belum profesional)

### Script cari leads:
1. Buka Google Maps di HP
2. Search "warung kopi bali" atau "cafe bali"
3. Klik satu-satu → cek rating & foto
4. Kalau belum punya logo bagus → catat nama + nomor WA
5. Kirim template WhatsApp #1
